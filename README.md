# Job Market Analyzer 📊

A data analysis project exploring 61,953 real job postings scraped from Google Search, uncovering trends in the data industry.

## Key Findings
- **SQL** is the #1 most in-demand skill, appearing in nearly 30,000 postings
- **Data Analyst** is the most common job title with 6,000+ postings
- The market is nearly split — **54.8% on-site** vs **45.2% remote**
- **Upwork** leads all companies in job postings, reflecting strong freelance demand

## Analysis Includes
- Top 10 most common job titles
- Top 10 hiring companies
- Remote vs on-site breakdown
- Top 20 most in-demand technical skills
- Job schedule type distribution

## Tech Stack
- **Python 3.13**
- **Pandas** — data loading and manipulation
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn** — data visualization
- **Jupyter Notebook** — analysis environment

## Dataset
[Data Analyst Job Postings on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lukebarousse/data-analyst-job-postings-google-search) — 61,953 postings, 27 features
